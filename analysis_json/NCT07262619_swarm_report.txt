## Clinical Trial Protocol Extraction and Analysis Report

**Trial ID:** NCT07262619
**Title:** EIK1005-002: A Clinical Research Study Evaluating EIK1005, a Werner Helicase Inhibitor, as Monotherapy and in Combination With Pembrolizumab in Participants With Advanced Solid Tumors Including Microsatellite Instability High (MSI-H) Tumors
**Status:** Extracted and ready for downstream analysis.

---

### 1. Raw Protocol Design Characteristics (Extracted)

The trial is a **Phase 1/Phase 2 Adaptive Design** studying the agent **EIK1005** (a Werner Helicase Inhibitor) as monotherapy and in combination with Pembrolizumab in patients with advanced solid tumors, specifically targeting **Microsatellite Instability High (MSI-H) Tumors**.

#### A. Study Design
*   **Design Type:** Adaptive Design (Type: Dose-Finding / Phase I).
*   **Control Type:** Standard of Care.
*   **Randomization:** Stratified by Baseline, Prior Treatment, and Age.
*   **Interim Analysis/Stopping Rules:** Not explicitly mentioned in the protocol text.

#### B. Population & Eligibility Criteria Summary
*   **Target Population:** Patients with advanced, unresectable, and/or metastatic solid tumors, specifically those with locally confirmed **MSI-H or dMMR** tumors.
*   **Key Inclusion Criteria:**
    *   Age $\ge 18$ years, providing informed consent.
    *   Life expectancy of at least 3 months.
    *   Archival tumor tissue $\le 3$ years old for retrospective confirmation of MSI-H/dMMR.
    *   Measurable disease at baseline per RECIST 1.1.
    *   ECOG performance status of 0 or 1.
    *   Adequate organ and marrow function.
*   **Key Exclusion/Safety Criteria:**
    *   History of progression after prior standard treatment.
    *   Prior treatment with Werner (WRN) inhibitor.
    *   History of relevant drug hypersensitivity or serious allergic reactions.
    *   Diagnosis of immunodeficiency or receiving chronic systemic steroids within 7 days prior to dosing.
    *   Active tuberculosis or active systemic infections.
    *   Known additional malignancy progressing or requiring active treatment within the past 3 years.
    *   Known active Central Nervous System (CNS) metastases or carcinomatous meningitis.

#### C. Endpoints Summary
*   **Primary Endpoints (Safety/Dose-Finding):**
    1.  Dose-Limiting Toxicity (DLT) - Part 1 (21 Days).
    2.  Adverse Events (AEs) - Part 1 and Part 2.
*   **Secondary Endpoints (Efficacy/PK):**
    *   Objective Response (OR) and Disease Control (DC) (Surrogate endpoints).
    *   Progression-free survival (PFS) (Part 2).
    *   Pharmacokinetic (PK) parameters of EIK1005 (AUC0-24, AUCtau,ss, Cmax, tmax, t½).

#### D. Pharmacogenetics
*   **Biomarkers of Interest:** PD1, PDL1, CTLA4 (related to immunotherapy response).
*   **Screen Failure Risk:** Estimated $\sim 85\%$ screen failure rate for the dMMR/MSI-H biomarker criterion.
